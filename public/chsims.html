During gameplay, press Ctrl + Shift + Alt + C and enter any of the following "Codes":

Cheats Comment
klapaucius   1000 Simoleons
water_tool   Make Your Home an Island
set_hour #   Change Time of Day to # (1-24)
set_speed #   Change Game Speed to # (-1000-1000)
interests   Change Personality and Interests of Your Sims
autonomy #   Change How Sims Think on Their Own (1-100)
grow_grass #   Grow Grass # (1-150)
map_edit on/off   Toggle Edit Map
route_balloons on/off   Toggle Basic Tutorial
sweep on/off   Show Ticks of the Game Toggle
tile_info on/off   Toggle Show/Hide Tile Info
log_mask   Set Event Logging Mask
draw_all_frames on/off   Toggle Draw All Frames
! Repeat Last Cheat
; Seperate Multiple Cheat Commands
move_objects on/off Enable/Disable moving objects (i.e. meal , water , mailbox , sims , cars)
