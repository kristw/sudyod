Winning Eleven 4

Bonus teams:
Beat the game with all the league and cups on the hard difficulty setting. The European All-Star Team, World All-Star Team, Classic European All-Star Team, and Classic World All-Star Team will be unlocked. Complete the Olympics with Japan to unlock the Old Japan team.

Bonus cup:
Win all the normal cups to unlock a bonus cup.

Bonus stadium:
Win the Konami Cup on any difficulty setting to unlock the Clubhouse stadium in exhibition mode.
