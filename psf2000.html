FIFA 2000

Ronaldo and Pele:
For some reason the two Brazilian geniuses are absent, and replaced with others. In the Inter Milan team, where Ronaldo should be is a player called No. 9, and in the classic 1970 Brazil team, Pele is replaced with No 10.

Robbie Williams:
The singer who contributed a song to this game's soundtrack can be found playing for the team 'Special Guests', found in the Rest of the World Section.

Special Teams:
Several special teams can be found in the Rest of the World section, four EA teams (EA1 etc), a Special Guests team and Port Vale (who Robbie Williams supports)
